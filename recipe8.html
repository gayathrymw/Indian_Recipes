Ingredients:

For the Batter:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the soaked ingredients and grind them to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Ferment the batter in a warm place for 8-12 hours until it doubles in volume.
  4. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into them.
  5. Steam the idlis in a steamer or pressure cooker without the weight for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Remove the idlis from the molds and serve hot with sambar and chutney.

Tips:

  • Ensure the batter is well fermented for soft idlis.
  • You can add grated carrots or spinach to the batter for added nutrition.
