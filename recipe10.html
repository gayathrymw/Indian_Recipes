Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Syrup:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon rose water

For the Gulab Jamun:

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. To make the syrup, combine sugar, water, and cardamom powder in a saucepan. Boil until the sugar dissolves. Add rose water and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine khoya, all-purpose flour, and baking powder. Mix well and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls, ensuring they are smooth and crack-free.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown, ensuring even cooking.
  5. Remove the fried balls and soak them in the warm syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature, garnished with nuts if desired.

Tips:

  • Ensure the oil is not too hot; fry on low heat for even cooking.
  • You can use ready-made gulab jamun mix if you're short on time.
