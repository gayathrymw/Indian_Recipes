Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups spinach (palak), blanched and pureed
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add spinach puree, spices, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and gently mix. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, add a little cream before serving.
  • You can also add a pinch of kasuri methi for enhanced taste.
