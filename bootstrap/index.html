Acid-Base Titration

This experiment determines the concentration of an unknown acid using a base solution of known concentration.

Mathematical Equation

The titration equation: MaVa = MbVb

Where M is molarity and V is volume for acid (a) and base (b).

pOH = -log[OH-]


Chemical Equation

The general acid-base neutralization reaction:

H+ + OH- → H2O

HCl + NaOH → NaCl + H2O


Conclusion

Acid-base titration is a fundamental technique in chemistry for determining unknown concentrations.
