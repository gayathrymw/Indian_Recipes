Aloo Paratha Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

For the Filling:

  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Cover and set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. When they start to crackle, add mashed potatoes and spices. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes. Let it cool.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll out a ball of dough, place the potato filling in the center, and seal the edges.
  4. Roll out the stuffed dough gently and cook on a hot tawa with oil until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with butter, yogurt, or pickle.

Tips:

  • Make sure the filling is dry to prevent the paratha from breaking while rolling.
  • Serve with a dollop of butter for authentic taste.
