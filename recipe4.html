Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Marinade:

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

For the Skewers:

  • 1 bell pepper, cut into squares
  • 1 onion, cut into squares
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, salt, and oil to form a marinade.
  2. Add the paneer cubes, bell pepper, and onion to the marinade. Ensure all pieces are well coated. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Thread the marinated paneer, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers alternately.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill or in the oven. Cook for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the paneer is golden and slightly charred.
  6. Remove from heat and sprinkle fresh coriander leaves on top for garnish.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
