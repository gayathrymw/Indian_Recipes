Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup semolina
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

For the Chole:

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon chole masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. For the bhature, mix flour, semolina, baking powder, baking soda, and yogurt. Knead into a smooth dough and let it rest for 2 hours.
  2. Roll out small portions of the dough and deep fry in hot oil until golden and puffed up. Drain on a paper towel.
  3. For the chole, pressure cook the chickpeas with salt until soft. Set aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add tomato puree, spices, and cooked chickpeas. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve hot with bhature, garnished with coriander and onion rings.

Tips:

  • Add a pinch of baking soda to the chickpeas while cooking to soften them faster.
  • Bhature dough should be well-kneaded for fluffy results.
