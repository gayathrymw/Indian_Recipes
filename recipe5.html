Ingredients:

For the Dosa Batter:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as necessary.
  3. Let the batter ferment in a warm place for 8-10 hours.
  4. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat. Grease it lightly with oil or ghee.
  6. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it out in a circular motion to form a thin layer.
  7. Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy. Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Tips:

  • Ensure the batter is well fermented for a crispy dosa.
  • You can add a little water to adjust the consistency of the batter if it's too thick.
